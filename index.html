Welcome to my GitHub Pages.

I'm a Distinguished Professor and Doctoral Supervisor in the College of Biomedical Engineering at Shenzhen University.

My research focuses on medical artificial intelligence, with over twenty years of experience spanning mathematical modeling theory, open-source algorithm software, clinical applications, and industrial transformation. I have developed open-source software that has become a core interactive segmentation component of 3D Slicer, the world's largest open-source medical image computing software, used by researchers from multiple countries in their research and clinical experiments.
